Privacy Policy - Todo.txt for Android

Aditya Bhaskar · 2020.06.08

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install and use ‘Todo.txt for Android’ (the “App”).

PERSONAL INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We do not use Google Analytics, or any other analytics tools to track usage of the App.

When you log-in to the App, we are provided certain information by the login provider - Dropbox or Google (the “File Providers”). This includes information about your display name, email address and profile photo. We refer to this automatically-collected information as “Profile Information.”

When you make a subscription purchase through the App, Google may collect certain information from you, including your name, billing address, billing zip code, shipping address, payment information (including credit card numbers), email address, and phone number. Google only shares the billing zip code and country name with us - we refer to this information as “Order Information.”

For supporters, the app may store an irreversible, encrypted hash of your email address and File Provider name, alongside your subscription order number to our server to enable syncing subscriptions with the Todo.txt extension for Chrome. The email address is encrypted in the App before transmission to the server so that the server never receives the real email address. We cannot decrypt the email address from the stored hash. We refer to this information as “Subscription Syncing Information.”

When we talk about “Personal Information” in this Privacy Policy, we are talking about all — Profile Information, Order Information, and Subscription Syncing Information.

HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

We do not use the Order Information that we collect generally. We may use this Order Information if we are contacted by you, the user, for confirmation or cancellation of an order.

Your Profile Information may be used to pre-fill fields in the feedback form - only when you use it from within the App. This may be changed or removed by you before submitting the feedback form.

We do not use the Profile Information outside of the App for any other purposes.

The Subscription Syncing Information is only used for syncing subscription status across Todo.txt apps for Android and Chrome. We do not use this information for any other purpose outside the app.

SHARING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We do not share your Personal Information with anyone.

The File Providers may collect your information from logins, file syncs and other interactions. This information is not controlled or collected by the App.

  • If connecting to Dropbox - you can read more about how Dropbox uses your Personal Information here.
  • If connecting to Google Drive – you can read more about how Google uses your Personal Information here.

Subscription purchase inside the App is provided by Google Pay. You can read more about how Google uses your Order Information here.

YOUR RIGHTS

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information below.

DATA RETENTION

We do not collect or store any Profile Information outside the App. Any Profile Information provided when you login to the App is deleted when you logout from the App, or remove the App.

When you place an order through the App, we will maintain your Order Information for our records unless and until you ask us to delete this information.

CHANGES

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

CONTACT US

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by using the contact form here.
